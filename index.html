Welcome!

I’m Marco Pasquato, an UX designer with a graphic design background, among other things.

Here you can find some projects I’m working on. I decided to make most of them freely available so that anyone can enjoy editing and using them.

Human progress stems from knowledge, and that’s why it should be open to anyone.

This website is in a never-ending construction phase, so new projects might be hosted here in the future.

Scroll down and feel free to have a look!
